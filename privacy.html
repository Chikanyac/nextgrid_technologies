Privacy Policy

At NextGrid Technologies (Pty) Ltd (“NextGrid”, “we”), we respect your privacy and process personal information in line with POPIA. Questions? Contact info@nextgridtechnologies.co.za or 076 948 9154. Information Officer: Clem Chikanya.

1. What we collect

  • Contact details you provide via forms, WhatsApp (076 948 9154), email, calls
  • Business info: company name, number of users, M365 tenant ID (for audits)
  • Support logs: device health, threat alerts, backup status (for managed clients)
  • Website analytics: anonymized IP, pages visited (via privacy-friendly analytics)

2. How we use it

  • To deliver IT security & support, R950 checks, quotes
  • To send monthly health reports if you are a managed client
  • To comply with law and POPIA retention
  • We do NOT sell data, we do NOT spam

3. Legal basis (POPIA Sec 11)

Consent, contract (to provide services), legitimate interest (security), legal obligation.

4. Sharing

We share only with: Microsoft (for M365 licensing), our PSA/RMM (for ticketing/monitoring) under DPA, and SA data centres for offsite backup. No cross-border transfer without your consent, except Microsoft 365 (EU/US with SCCs).

5. Security measures (Condition 7)

  • MFA enforced for all staff, Conditional Access
  • Encryption at rest and in transit, BitLocker on devices
  • Access on need-to-know, audit logs, DLP
  • Backups immutable, tested weekly

6. Retention

Support logs: 3 years. Invoices: 5 years per Tax Admin Act. M365 audit logs: 1 year. You may request deletion earlier unless law requires retention.

7. Your rights (Sec 5, 23, 24)

Access, correction, deletion, objection, complain to Information Regulator. Email info@nextgridtechnologies.co.za with ID verification. We respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

Only essential cookies for site function. No tracking ads.
