4. Security Measures (POPIA Condition 7 & Sec 19) - What we actually do

Identity & Access

  • MFA number matching enforced for all staff (no SMS)
  • Conditional Access: block legacy auth, outside SA, impossible travel
  • Least privilege, quarterly access review, leaver process 24h

Encryption & DLP

  • BitLocker on all endpoints, TLS 1.2+ in transit
  • DLP for ID numbers, bank accounts leaving org
  • Sensitivity labels: Confidential → encrypt + no forward

Endpoint & Network

  • Defender for Business with ransomware rollback
  • Firewall IPS, WPA3 WiFi, guest isolated, no RDP open
  • Patches within 7 days critical, 30 days others

Backup & Logging

  • Immutable backups SA DC, weekly restore tests, 30+ day versioning
  • Audit logs M365 unified log 1 year, alert policies (mass delete, forwarding rule)
  • Incident plan tested quarterly
