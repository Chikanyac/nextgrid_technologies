3. Security Measures (POPIA Sec 19 & Condition 7) - Technical & Organisational

We implement appropriate, reasonable technical & organisational measures to prevent loss, damage, unauthorised access:

A. Identity - Zero Trust

  • Entra ID MFA number matching (no SMS bypass), 100% coverage, break-glass accounts excluded
  • Conditional Access: block legacy auth (SMTP AUTH, IMAP), block outside SA, block risky sign-ins, require compliant device for admin
  • Privileged Access Management: PIM for Global Admin, 24h max, approval required

B. Email & Data Protection

  • Defender for Office: Safe Links time-of-click rewrite, Safe Attachments sandbox detonation, Anti-phish impersonation for CEO/finance
  • SPF hard fail, DKIM 2048-bit, DMARC reject p=reject, monitored via aggregate reports
  • DLP: regex for SA ID, bank account, medical file blocking, plus sensitivity labels (Confidential = encrypt + watermark + no forward)

C. Endpoint & Network

  • Defender for Business EDR with ransomware rollback, attack surface reduction rules
  • Firewall: IPS, no RDP 3389 open to internet, firmware patched within 7 days critical
  • WiFi WPA3, guest VLAN isolated, default passwords changed, UPS for load-shedding

D. Backup, Logging, Organisational

  • 3-2-1 backup: M365 backup immutable SA DC (JHB/CPT), NAS local, weekly restore test documented
  • Audit: Unified Audit Log 1 year, Alert policies (mass delete, eDiscovery, forwarding rule, inbox rule), monthly report
  • Training: staff POPIA + phishing sim monthly, least privilege, clean desk, leaver checklist 24h, incident plan tabletop quarterly
Third-party audit: Secure Score 92/100 target, annual penetration test by external, results available under NDA. Call 076 948 9154 for summary.

5. Breach Notification - Sec 22 & 26

We monitor 24/7 via Defender alerts, RMM, audit log alerts (mass delete, eDiscovery, forwarding rule creation). If we suspect breach that may cause harm:

  • Internal: Contain (disable account, block IP, isolate device), assess scope, preserve logs.
  • Notify you (if you are affected client) within 24 hours of discovery via phone 076 948 9154 + email, with: what happened, what info affected, what we did, what you should do (reset password, watch phishing).
  • Notify Regulator within 72 hours per Sec 22 if meets threshold, via Form 2.
  • Notify data subjects if high risk, per Sec 22(2), unless law enforcement requests delay.
  • We keep breach register per Sec 26. You can request summary (redacted).
Emergency? If you suspect breach of your M365/business: call 076 948 9154 immediately (24/7 incident line), then email info@nextgridtechnologies.co.za with subject URGENT BREACH. We respond within 1 hour avg.
