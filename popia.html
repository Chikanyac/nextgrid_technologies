2. Definitions – Plain English for Auditors & Staff

SA SME context: If you support M365, you already handle personal information daily. Here’s what POPIA terms mean at NextGrid.

Personal Information

Any info that can identify you – name, email, cell, ID number, bank details, device serial, IP, even voice when you call 076 948 9154. Example: tumi@client.co.za + Intune DeviceID.

Data Subject

The person the info is about – your client contact, their staff member, our website visitor. Not the company itself.

Responsible Party

Decides why and how to process info. NextGrid for our own CRM/billing. Your company for your staff emails.

Operator

Processes info on behalf of Responsible Party under contract. NextGrid when we manage your tenant. Must have same security as you.

Processing

Anything we do: collect, store, use, share (with your consent), backup, delete. Viewing a support ticket = processing.

Special Personal Information

Sensitive: health, biometrics, criminal, political. We AVOID collecting this unless required for access (e.g., face ID to unlock device, we don’t store biometric template).

Information Officer

Person accountable for compliance. Registered with Regulator. For NextGrid: Clem Chikanya, 076 948 9154.

Personal Information Impact Assessment

Check before new risky processing. We do one before onboarding new RMM or moving backups offsite during loadshedding.

8. Security Measures Deep Dive – Technical & Organisational (Sec 19)

What we deploy for you is what we use ourselves. Fixed price includes these – no “POPIA add-on” surprise. On-site Pretoria & Centurion engineers follow same checklist as remote across SA.

Technical Measures – What blocks hackers
Identity & Access – MFA number matching everywhere
  • MFA enforced via Microsoft Authenticator number match + Conditional Access: block legacy auth, block logins outside SA unless travel approved, require compliant device.
  • Least privilege – engineer gets JIT admin via PIM, expires in 4hrs. Quarterly access review – we show you report.
  • Passwordless where possible, SSPR self-service reduces helpdesk sharing passwords over 076 948 9154.
Email & Data Protection – DLP for SA specifics
  • Safe Links, Safe Attachments, anti-phish policies – quarantine, not just junk.
  • DLP policies: detect SA ID numbers (13 digits + Luhn), bank accounts, credit cards – block external send, encrypt automatically.
  • Retention labels: 3y support, 5y finance auto-applied. Can’t delete override without compliance admin.
Devices & Network – survive loadshedding
  • Intune compliance: BitLocker enforced, PIN 6+, encryption on, defender up-to-date, OS patched within 14 days.
  • EDR (Defender for Business/Huntress) with rollback – isolate device in 90 seconds, auto rollback ransomware.
  • WPA3 WiFi, firewall IPS, UPS 2KVA + generator at office, Azure replication so backups never stop during Stage 4-6.
Backups & Recovery – immutable, SA, tested
  • 3-2-1-1 rule: 3 copies, 2 media, 1 offsite SA DC, 1 immutable. Weekly automated restore test + report.
  • Encryption AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.2 in transit. Keys in Azure Key Vault, not Excel.
  • Audit logs: M365 Purview 1 year searchable, RMM logs 3 years.

Training & Culture
  • All staff POPIA induction + quarterly phishing simulation. If you click sim, extra training same week. We track click-rate – target <5%.

Contracts & Vetting
  • Operator agreements (DPA) with Microsoft, PSA/RMM vendors under SCCs. Staff background checks, NDA signed.

9. Breach Notification Procedure – 72hr clock (Sec 22)

We monitor 24/7 via Defender + SOC alerts. If something looks like personal info loss (e.g., mailbox compromised, device stolen, ransomware), we start clock immediately. Plain English, no hiding.

0-1hr • Detect & Log

Alert fires → engineer triages → creates P1 incident → notifies Information Officer Clem Chikanya.

Tool: Defender, Huntress, RMM
1-4hr • Contain & Preserve

Isolate device/account, reset creds, block IP, snapshot logs (don’t delete evidence). Call affected client contact.

4-24hr • Assess & Notify You

Assess: what info? whose? how many? Risk of harm? If likely to cause harm, we notify you within 24h of discovery with facts + what to do. Phone 076 948 9154 + email.

72hr • Regulator

We file Form – Notification of Security Compromise to Information Regulator (complaints.IR@justice.gov.za) within 72h if required. Include: nature, categories, approx subjects, consequences, mitigation.

Post • Lessons & Close

Root cause, remediation (e.g., enforce MFA number matching), customer letter, update register. Keep record 5 years.

What the breach notice we send YOU includes (minimum)
  • Date/time discovered + how we found it
  • Description of info involved (e.g., names + emails, not ID if not)
  • Number of data subjects affected (estimate)
  • Likely consequences – e.g., phishing risk
  • What we already did – isolate, reset
  • What you should do – warn staff, reset passwords, watch bank
  • Contact: Information Officer Clem Chikanya – 076 948 9154 & info@nextgridtechnologies.co.za
  • Regulator reference if filed
Emergency breach line: Always 076 948 9154 – leave voicemail with “BREACH” and we callback within 1hr 24/7. Also WhatsApp wa.me/27769489154 with BREACH keyword escalates.
